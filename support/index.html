Getting the Best Results

  • Place the iPhone or iPad on a stand facing your child, around arm's length away.
  • Good lighting on your child's face helps the camera detect chewing.
  • If the camera can't see the face, MunchGuard falls back to private on-device audio.
  • Use the "Show live monitoring details" toggle on the dashboard to see what the detector senses.
  • Adjust the grace period and sensitivity (Easy / Normal / Strict) in Session Setup for your child.

Approving Videos

  • Open Manage approved library on the parent dashboard.
  • Add a video by pasting an exact YouTube video link you already trust.
  • MunchGuard does not provide open YouTube search or channel browsing to children.
  • Remove anything anytime with the (–) button. Kids only ever see what you've approved.

Permissions & Privacy

MunchGuard uses the camera and microphone during a meal to detect chewing on your device. Optional Premium meal recording creates a temporary local video/audio file only when a parent turns it on; a parent saves, exports, or discards it after the meal. Optional Sign in with Apple is parent-facing and used only for private iCloud backup and restore. There is no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Common Topics

  • Why does the video pause? With no chewing detected, MunchGuard pauses by design — a few steady bites resumes it.
  • Allowing camera, microphone, and (optional) motion access.
  • Choosing content types to organize approved videos.
  • Reading the post-meal summary and chew streaks.
  • Deleting all local data by removing the app.
