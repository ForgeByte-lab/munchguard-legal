MunchGuard

Parent-approved mealtime videos that play while kids chew and gently pause when bites stop.

App Facts

PlatformiPhone and iPad
AvailabilitySubmitted to Apple App Review
Price30 days free, then $0.99/month or $19.99 lifetime

Short Description

MunchGuard helps parents turn mealtime videos into a calmer routine. Parents approve the exact videos and channels their child can watch, then MunchGuard plays them in a built-in iPhone/iPad player that stays on while chewing continues and pauses with a friendly dino reminder when chewing stops.

Privacy

Chewing detection runs on-device during active meals. Camera and microphone input is not recorded, saved, or uploaded. MunchGuard has no ads and no third-party analytics SDKs.
