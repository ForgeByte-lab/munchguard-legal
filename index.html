Mealtime videos that play only while your child is chewing.

MunchGuard turns mealtimes into a gentle game. The video plays while your child eats and pauses with a friendly reminder when they stop — and you approve every exact video they can see.

What MunchGuard Helps You Do

  • Build a closed, parent-approved library from exact video links you already choose — paste a link, or share one to MunchGuard from the YouTube app or Safari.
  • Let the front camera and microphone notice chewing on-device — chewing plays, stopping pauses with a friendly reminder.
  • Keep kids in a safe, built-in player with no search, no recommendations, and no rabbit holes.
  • See a friendly post-meal summary with watch time and chew streaks, and pick a chew-buddy character who eats along with your child.
  • Enjoy calmer mealtimes on iPhone or iPad, in your hands or on a stand.

Free & Premium

MunchGuard is free to try for 30 days. MunchGuard Premium then unlocks the full experience:

  • Connect your own YouTube account to import playlists and liked videos you already curated (read-only — MunchGuard only ever sees videos you chose), so you can fill your approved library without pasting every link by hand.
  • Playlists, extra chew-buddy characters, buddy wardrobe, Kid Mode themes, and celebration styles.
  • Optional parent voice reminders and a calm-down breathing moment on the pause screen.
  • Optional local meal recording that stays on your device unless you choose to save or share it.
