Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

MunchGuard ("MunchGuard," "we," "our," or "us") is developed by Haider Ali. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MunchGuard mobile application (the "App"). MunchGuard is a parent-controlled mealtime video app designed for families. We built it to be private by default: no advertising, no tracking, no developer-operated analytics, and no developer-operated account database.

1. The Short Version

  • MunchGuard's chewing detection and child-facing video experience work on your device. We do not run ads, third-party analytics, or tracking.
  • Sign in with Apple is optional. If you choose to use it, your approved library and meal history can be backed up to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.
  • The camera and microphone are used only during a meal session to detect chewing, entirely on-device. Nothing they capture is recorded, saved, or transmitted.
  • We do not use ads, third-party analytics, advertising identifiers, or cross-app tracking, and we never sell data.

2. Camera, Microphone, and Motion

MunchGuard's core feature is "chew to watch": a video plays while your child is chewing and pauses when they stop. To do this, during an active meal session only:

  • Camera: the front camera is used with Apple's on-device face/landmark technology to detect jaw and mouth movement. Camera frames are processed live on the device and are never saved, photographed, recorded, or sent anywhere.
  • Microphone: audio is analyzed on-device for chewing rhythm as a backup when the face is turned away. Audio is never recorded, saved, or transmitted.
  • Motion (optional): if you use compatible AirPods or Beats, their motion sensors can help detect chewing. These samples are processed on-device and never saved or sent.

These sensors are active only while a meal session is running and stop when the session ends or the app leaves the screen.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The following is stored locally on your device so the app can work. It stays on your device and is not uploaded to us:

  • The videos and channels you approve, your app settings (such as meal length and sensitivity), and your selected age range.
  • Local meal summaries such as session length, a "mindful" score, and chew streaks.

You can remove approved videos and channels at any time inside the app. Deleting the App removes all of this local data from your device.

4. Optional Sign in with Apple and iCloud Backup

MunchGuard can use Sign in with Apple so a parent can restore family mealtime data across their own Apple devices. If you choose to sign in, the App may store your Sign in with Apple user identifier, approved videos, approved channels, settings, and meal-history summaries in your personal iCloud account through CloudKit. This backup is used only for app functionality and restore/sync. We do not receive your Apple ID password, payment card information, camera footage, microphone audio, or child-facing sensor data.

You can use MunchGuard without signing in. You can also delete local app data by removing the App from your device; iCloud data can be managed through your Apple account and iCloud settings.

5. Information We Do Not Collect

MunchGuard does not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • No MunchGuard account, separate username, or password is required.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, payment card, camera recordings, microphone recordings, or child biometric profile.
  • No advertising identifiers, no third-party advertising, and no third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No contact list, photos, browsing history, location, or health data is collected.

6. Watching YouTube Videos

The videos you approve are played through YouTube's official embedded player using the privacy-enhanced youtube-nocookie.com domain. When a video is playing, that playback is delivered by YouTube/Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). MunchGuard does not create a Google account session, does not sign your child in, and does not share any personal information with Google. We use the no-cookie domain specifically to reduce personalization during playback. Because MunchGuard is parent-controlled, children only ever see videos and channels a parent has approved.

7. Children's Privacy

MunchGuard is intended to be set up and managed by a parent or guardian and used by children under adult supervision. MunchGuard does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents control the entire content library. Optional Sign in with Apple and iCloud backup are parent-facing restore features; they are not child accounts.

8. App Store Purchases

MunchGuard Premium is offered through Apple's App Store. A monthly subscription may include a 30-day free trial and then renew at the listed App Store price unless canceled. A lifetime unlock may also be available as a one-time in-app purchase. Purchases, billing, refunds, subscription management, and payment details are handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

9. Data Security

Local data is protected by your device's own security (passcode, Face ID/Touch ID, and iOS data protection). If you enable iCloud backup, synced data is stored through Apple's iCloud/CloudKit systems for your Apple account.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
